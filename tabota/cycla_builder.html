The flattened file is the depth-1 onset list (the original flat .cyc) sampled at the current depth — it drops into TaboTa's existing importer. The grammar file keeps the recursion and depth-aware snapping.
Top band: the subdivision tree — each stratum is one level deeper, cells tinted by level. Bottom band: the indispensability ruler — every onset is a tick whose height and weight come from the shallowest level it appears at. The downbeat is tallest; deeper cuts are progressively lighter. Snapping in the roll would round to the nearest tick at or above the chosen depth.
