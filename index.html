Bio

Xyh Tamura is an interdisciplinary artist, singer-songwriter, and researcher engaging with posthumanities, techno-global ontologies, and vernacular cultures. They are a graduate of Physics from the University of the Philippines and are currently taking MA Creative Writing.

Currently Xyh's work involves experimental pop, intermedial and conceptual art, videopoetry, and electronic literature leveraging digital environments and multisensory experiences, often from a personal, Philippine (Ilonggo) and Japanese perspective and incorporating the sciences. They are researching the perception of tuning systems across cultures and developing post-MIDI technologies to support non-standard tuning systems in music. They are also doing research on how technology shapes ontologies, and how global, subcultural, and vernacular life-worlds evolve alongside technology and globalization.
